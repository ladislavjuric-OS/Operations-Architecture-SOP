# Launch Day Operations
## Hour-by-Hour D-Day Execution Playbook

> "Launch day is not when the work starts. It's when the preparation is tested."

---

## Purpose

This is the operational playbook for the most critical 24-48 hours of any campaign or product launch. Every action, every channel, every team member ‚Äî mapped to a specific time slot.

Launch Day is not about creativity. It's about **execution discipline.** The strategy was decided weeks ago. Today is about hitting marks.

---

## Pre-Launch Checklist (L-24h)

Before the clock starts, confirm every system is armed:

### Technical
- [ ] Campaign/product page LIVE in draft/preview ‚Äî final visual check
- [ ] All links tested (perks, checkout, landing pages)
- [ ] Tracking verified: GA4, Pixel, UTM parameters firing correctly
- [ ] Email sequences loaded and scheduled (VIP, Broad, Urgency)
- [ ] Social media posts scheduled across all channels
- [ ] War Room dashboard live ‚Äî KPI tracking in real-time

### Team
- [ ] All team members briefed on hour-by-hour schedule
- [ ] Communication channel confirmed (Slack/WhatsApp/War Room)
- [ ] Roles confirmed: who handles what (see Role Matrix below)
- [ ] Emergency protocol reviewed (what if X breaks)

### Media & Partners
- [ ] PR embargo lift time confirmed with all outlets
- [ ] Influencer post times confirmed
- [ ] Cross-promotion partners briefed and scheduled
- [ ] Affiliate/referral links tested

## Emergency Protocols

### If page/platform goes down:
1. Immediately contact platform support
2. Redirect all traffic to landing page with email capture
3. Communicate transparently on social media
4. Document downtime for potential credit/extension request

### If conversion rate is below target by Hour 6:
1. Review page analytics ‚Äî where are visitors dropping off?
2. Check pricing ‚Äî is the value proposition clear?
3. Test alternative hero image or headline
4. Increase paid ad budget on best-performing audience
5. Deploy founder personal outreach to warm leads

### If Day 1 target is clearly unreachable by Hour 12:
1. Shift narrative from "Day 1 goal" to "first 48 hours"
2. Extend early bird deadline by 24h (creates second urgency wave)
3. Activate backup outreach channels
4. Document lessons for campaign strategy adjustment

## War Room Dashboard ‚Äî What to Track in Real-Time

1. **Funding total** (live counter)
2. **Backer count** (live counter)
3. **Conversion rate** (visitors ‚Üí backers)
4. **Traffic by source** (UTM breakdown)
5. **Email performance** (opens, clicks, conversions per blast)
6. **Ad spend vs. revenue** (real-time ROAS)
7. **Top referring sources** (where are backers coming from)
8. **Comment/question volume** (community health indicator)
9. **Refund requests** (early warning signal)
10. **Velocity trend** (pledges per hour ‚Äî is momentum building or declining?)
