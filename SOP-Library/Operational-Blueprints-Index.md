## 1. Nashville Architecture‚Ñ¢

**Purpose:** Single source of truth ‚Äî every process, every KPI, every owner documented and traceable.

**Core principles:**
- One system of record (no parallel spreadsheets)
- Every task has an owner (if nobody owns it, it doesn't exist)

## 2. Spoke-and-Hub Model

**Purpose:** Multi-divisional resource allocation without context-switching friction.

**The structure:** Central Hub (founder ‚Äî strategy + capital) connects to independent Spokes (each with own P&L, KPIs, and team lead). Bridge SOPs define how spokes share resources. Kill Criteria define when a spoke gets shut down ‚Äî numbers decide, not feelings.

**Application:** Originally designed for Venture Studio / Agency hybrids. Now applied to any founder running 2+ business units.

## 6. The Accountability Rhythm

**Purpose:** Replace ad-hoc firefighting with a predictable cadence.

| Frequency | Focus |
|---|---|
| **Daily** | Blockers, priorities, handoffs (10-15 min) |
| **Weekly** | KPI status, red items, decisions (30-45 min) |
| **Monthly** | Trend analysis, resource allocation (60-90 min) |
| **Quarterly** | OKRs, kill/continue decisions, 90-day sprints (half day) |

**Rule:** If a meeting doesn't produce a decision or surface a blocker, it shouldn't exist.
